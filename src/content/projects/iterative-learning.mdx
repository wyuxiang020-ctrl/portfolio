## Premise
<span class="h-sub">项目缘起</span>

如果一栋建筑的空间逻辑能够体现学习本身的过程，那会是什么样的建筑？曼彻斯特城市大学的信息技术大楼（ITB）已经废弃——最初的功能在云端时代早已失效。我们面对的问题是：它应该成为什么？

我们的提案将其改造为研究生学生活动中心，但这栋建筑不只是*承载*学习——它*演绎*学习。

<details>
<summary>Read in English</summary>

What if a building's spatial logic could embody the very process of learning? The ITB (Information Technology Building) at Manchester Metropolitan University sits abandoned — its original function rendered obsolete by the cloud. We were given the question: what should it become?

Our proposal transforms it into a postgraduate student hub, but the architecture does not simply *accommodate* learning. It *enacts* it.

</details>

## The Structural Analogy
<span class="h-sub">结构类比</span>

项目建立在三重结构类比之上：

- **学习心理学** — 知识建立在先验知识之上；每一次新的相遇都在重塑此前所有的积累
- **计算机迭代** — 一个在每次循环中精炼输出的循环，从不抵达固定的终点
- **游戏机制** — 通过失败获得进展、反馈循环，以及可选的探索路径

三种系统共享同一种几何形态：**循环**。不是大多数教育建筑的走廊加房间式图解，而是一条连续回路——其中没有任何时刻是死胡同。

<details>
<summary>Read in English</summary>

The project draws on a three-way structural analogy:

- **Learning psychology** — the idea that knowledge builds on prior knowledge; each new encounter reshapes what came before
- **Computational iteration** — a loop that refines its output with each pass, never arriving at a fixed endpoint
- **Game mechanics** — progress through failure, feedback loops, and optional exploration paths

These three systems share a geometry: *the loop*. Not the corridor-and-room diagram of most educational buildings, but a continuous circuit where no moment is a dead end.

</details>

## Spatial Strategy
<span class="h-sub">空间策略</span>

既有楼板全部保留，并通过一条新的竖向交通核心串联——半为楼梯，半为展廊，半为停顿。每层对应一种不同的学习模式：

- **地面层** — 邂逅、到达、偶然（开放且可渗透）
- **一层** — 专注个人工作（声学隔离）
- **二层** — 协作工作室（共享基础设施）
- **三层** — 汇报与反思（阶梯剖面）

> 这栋建筑不告诉你该去哪里。它给你探索的理由。
>
> *The building doesn't tell you where to go. It gives you reasons to explore.*

<details>
<summary>Read in English</summary>

The existing building's floor plates are retained and connected by a new vertical circulation spine — part staircase, part exhibition gallery, part pause. Each level corresponds to a different mode of learning:

- **Ground** — encounter, arrival, serendipity (open and permeable)
- **First** — focused individual work (acoustic separation)
- **Second** — collaborative studio (shared infrastructure)
- **Third** — presentation and reflection (amphitheatre section)

</details>

## Material Palette
<span class="h-sub">材料策略</span>

我们保留了既有混凝土结构，并刻意将其裸露——包浆作为时间的证据。新置入的部分使用温暖的桦木胶合板与哑光钢材，在新旧之间建立清晰的可读性，而不消除它们之间的对话。

<details>
<summary>Read in English</summary>

We retained the existing concrete structure and exposed it deliberately — patina as evidence of time. New insertions are in warm birch plywood and matte steel, creating a clear legibility between old and new without erasing the dialogue between them.

</details>
