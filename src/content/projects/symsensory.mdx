## 概述 · Overview

项目位于重庆西部（重庆）科学城大学城板块，是面向在校学生、办公人群、游客与周边居民的综合型创业中心。设计从**品牌营销**的视角出发，将"新五感"——氛围感、放松感、社交感、存在感、原创感——引入建筑设计方法论，以感知体验为核心重新定义办公与创业空间的形态。

设计借助 **Grasshopper 参数化平台**中的 Wool Algorithm，分析不同使用者在场地中的行进路径与聚集节点，以此驱动功能分区与建筑形体的生成逻辑，将算法理性与空间体验融合为一体。

The project is located in the University Town of Western (Chongqing) Science City — a comprehensive entrepreneurship centre serving students, office workers, tourists, and residents. The design introduces a *New Five Senses* methodology — atmosphere, relaxation, social, presence, originality — as the spatial organising logic, departing from brand marketing thinking to redefine creative and entrepreneurial space.

A **Grasshopper Wool Algorithm** drives the design: tracing crowd paths and gathering nodes across the site to generate functional zoning and building form, integrating algorithmic rationality with lived spatial experience.

## 场地分析 · Site Analysis

场地位于重庆市沙坪坝区大学城板块，地处高新区核心地带。重庆大学城汇聚了重庆大学、重庆师范大学、四川美术学院等多所院校，拥有丰富的知识人才资源，地铁一号线贯穿其中，交通便捷。项目用地紧邻重庆师范大学，北可达大学城中心路，东临西大学城路，周边商业、住宅与高校用地交织，具备良好的人流基础。

场地内设有功能分区与道路划分两套系统，以高新区知识型人才与大学城多元使用者为主要服务对象，以建立兼顾生产、孵化与休闲的复合型园区为目标。

The site sits within Chongqing's University Town, a knowledge-intensive district clustering multiple universities including Chongqing University, Chongqing Normal University, and Sichuan Fine Arts Institute, connected by Subway Line 1. The plot borders Chongqing Normal University, with University Town Central Road to the north and West University City Road to the east. Commercial, residential, and academic uses converge around the site.

Two internal systems — functional zoning and road division — structure the campus, serving the district's knowledge workers and the university town's diverse users.

## 设计概念 · New Five Senses

"新五感"是本项目的核心设计方法论，源自品牌营销领域的感知研究，被系统转译为建筑空间的体验框架。五种感知维度各自对应特定的空间策略，共同构成创业中心的体验逻辑。

**氛围感 Atmosphere** — 建筑能够创造氛围，氛围被使用者感知。设计将空间的情感温度作为目标，以"第六感"与"有意义的整体"为引导，营造具有感染力的场所精神。

**放松感 Sense of Relaxation** — 关注内外部环境的协调。内部设置共享办公、户外剧场、涂鸦区等非正式功能；外部引入生态景观，形成人与空间之间自然而持续的对话。

**社交感 Sense of Social** — 通过场域关系、社会认同与社交空间形式三个层次，构建以自然场景为底色的社会性场所，鼓励使用者之间真实而有温度的相遇。

**存在感 Sense of Presence** — 通过独特的空间特征与技术和艺术的结合，建立个性化的使用体验。代表性空间节点包括吊床区、图书角与特色桌台，形成具有辨识度的品牌形象。

**原创感 Sense of Originality** — 强调场地自身的唯一性，以使用者对路径的自然选择作为空间生成的起点，将品牌身份识别（original IP）与建筑设计逻辑（wool algorithm）整合为一体。

*The New Five Senses* methodology translates brand perception research into architectural space. Each dimension — atmosphere, relaxation, social, presence, originality — maps to a specific spatial strategy, collectively forming the experiential framework of the entrepreneurship centre.

## 人群分析 · People Analysis

场地周边使用人群涵盖四类主要群体，各有其独特的年龄结构、行为模式与空间诉求。**学生（18–32岁）**是最主要的使用群体，活动需求涵盖购物、学习、社交与娱乐；**办公人群（25–60岁）**以舒适高效的工作环境为核心需求；**游客（10–75岁）**人数与流量随时段变化显著，对绿地与公共交通需求较强；**居民（0–75岁）**年龄跨度最广，青少年偏好社交，老年人以散步休憩为主，对绿化环境要求最高。

The four user groups span a wide range of ages and spatial needs — students, office workers, tourists, and residents each bring distinct behaviour patterns, movement rhythms, and programme requirements, demanding a building capable of hosting both focused work and casual leisure simultaneously.

## 羊毛算法 · Wool Algorithm

**羊毛算法（Wool Algorithm）**是一种基于分形长度与引力场的路径筛选方法，通过模拟人群在场地中自然行进的轨迹，识别出具有代表性的移动路径与关键聚集节点。

算法的核心逻辑分三步展开：首先，根据分形长度与引力球范围，从大量路径中筛选出两条最适合的羊毛路径；其次，在此基础上进一步提取重复路径与引力点，识别出真正具有设计价值的流线骨架；最终，将筛选后的路径整合为一张适用于本场地的新路径图，作为后续设计的空间生成依据。

*The Wool Algorithm* is a path-selection method based on fractional length and gravisphere simulation. It models natural crowd movement across the site in three stages: first, two optimal wool paths are selected from a large dataset based on fractional length and gravitational range; then, repeated paths and gravitational points are extracted to identify the core circulation skeleton; finally, the selected paths are integrated into a new site-specific path map that drives all subsequent spatial decisions.

![](/images/projects/symsensory/gallery-5.jpg)
<p class="caption">羊毛算法原理图解 · Wool Algorithm — 基于分形长度与引力场筛选路径，识别重复路径与引力点，最终生成适用于本场地的路径骨架 / Path selection based on fractional length and gravisphere: identifying repeated paths and gravitational points, then integrating them into a site-specific path skeleton</p>

在 **Grasshopper** 平台中，羊毛算法被嵌入完整的参数化工作流，实现从路径数据到建筑形态的连续迭代推演。设计以场地人群行为分析为输入，在 Grasshopper 中运行多轮路径模拟，逐步筛选、叠加、修正，最终确定驱动功能分区与体块组织的路径骨架。每一次迭代既是对人群行为数据的回应，也是对建筑功能逻辑的校验，两者相互反馈，共同推动设计向更合理的方向演化。

In **Grasshopper**, the Wool Algorithm is embedded within a continuous parametric workflow — translating path data into building form through iterative simulation. Crowd behaviour analysis provides the input; multiple rounds of path simulation in Grasshopper progressively filter, overlay, and refine the skeleton, with each iteration responding to movement data and verifying functional logic simultaneously. Design and data evolve together.

![](/images/projects/symsensory/gallery-6.jpg)
<p class="caption">Grasshopper 参数化路径迭代过程 · Grasshopper parametric path iteration — 从场地人群数据出发，在 GH 平台中多轮模拟筛选，逐步确定驱动功能分区与体块组织的最终路径骨架 / From crowd data input, through multiple simulation rounds in Grasshopper, progressively determining the path skeleton that drives functional zoning and massing</p>

## 形体生成 · Form Generation

以羊毛算法生成的路径骨架为基础，形体生成分步展开：首先沿路径方向组织功能分区，根据各功能的规模需求确定体块高度；随后将不同功能体块沿路径流线连接，形成整体流线型的建筑形态；再结合地形条件与轨道交通站点关系布置低层体量，完善周边绿化；最终通过局部开洞优化体块之间的空间关系，以多层次流线型铝制表皮完成建筑外观。整个推演过程将算法生成的路径理性，转化为可感知的建筑空间秩序。

Building on the Wool Algorithm path skeleton, form generation proceeds step by step: functional zones are organised along path directions, with block heights determined by programme scale; volumes are connected along flow lines to form a unified streamlined mass; low-rise elements are placed in response to terrain and the adjacent light rail station; partial voids refine inter-volume relationships; and a multi-level aluminium skin completes the building's identity. The process translates algorithmic path logic into perceivable spatial order.

## 爆炸分析 · Explosion Diagram

爆炸图将建筑拆解为多个层级，系统呈现各部分的构成逻辑：**结构层**展示梁柱体系与基础关系，揭示建筑骨架如何支撑流线型整体形态；**功能层**逐层标注不同楼层的使用分区变化，展现从地面公共活动区到上层专业功能区的垂直过渡；**表皮层**呈现部分外立面材料的构成方式，说明轻质铝制流线型表皮的覆盖逻辑与开窗关系。三个层级相互叠合，完整揭示了这座建筑在结构、功能与外观三个维度上的内在秩序。

The explosion diagram disassembles the building into multiple layers, revealing the organisational logic of each: the **structural layer** shows the beam-column system and foundation, exposing the skeleton that supports the streamlined massing; the **functional layer** annotates programme distribution across floors, showing the vertical transition from ground-level public activity to upper-level specialist use; the **skin layer** illustrates how the lightweight aluminium cladding wraps the form and relates to the window openings. All three layers together reveal the building's internal order across structure, programme, and surface.

## 构造与材料 · Construction & Materials

### 节点构造 · Construction Details

建筑的围护体系由**流线型铝制表皮、玻璃幕墙、通风百叶与吊顶系统**共同构成。剖面透视大样展示了表皮与室内吊顶之间的层次关系：外层铝板以多层次流线造型覆盖结构，内层设置独立吊顶系统，两者之间形成通风腔体，配合可调节百叶窗实现自然通风与遮阳的平衡。节点详图清晰呈现了各构造层的连接逻辑与材料交接方式，保证建筑在高技术感外观之下兼顾良好的物理性能。

The building envelope integrates **streamlined aluminium cladding, glass curtain wall, ventilation louvres, and a ceiling system**. The sectional perspective detail reveals the layered relationship between outer skin and interior ceiling: aluminium panels wrap the structure in multi-level streamlines, an independent ceiling system is mounted within, and the cavity between enables natural ventilation — balanced with adjustable louvres for shading. Construction details show the connection logic and material junctions across each layer.
